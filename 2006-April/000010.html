We are intercepting packet for destination port 80 at the end of our DSL pipe
(the UK have a number of law project to force ISPs to protect their customer
from "unlawful" content - welcome to 1984 ..)

Those packet are source and destination natted to a squid farm. Each squid
 proxy have a url rewriter which will query (passing though some cache) a
 postgresql DB, depending on the answer (allowed, deny, not classified yet) a
 page is served.

The classification it use a combination of pattern matching (similar to Dan's
guardian) and bayesian on url, html, text of html, meta content, plus a few
trick for search engine, etc.nat

If you want to look at a similar GPL soft you can look at "POESIA" but IMHO
 it is over engineered and not stable enough.
